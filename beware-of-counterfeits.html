How to avoid Kamagra counterfeits

When buying Kamagra online, it's important to know how to check the drug itself to make sure it's genuine. Here are some detailed tips to help you identify genuine Kamagra products and avoid counterfeits:

1. Check the packaging and the appearance of the tablets:

Carefully examine the outer packaging of the product to ensure that it is sealed and intact. Damaged or poorly sealed packaging could indicate a counterfeit. Next, examine the tablets themselves. Genuine Kamagra tablets are usually rectangular in shape with rounded edges and are marked "KGR" followed by the strength (for example, "100" for 100mg or "50" for 50mg). Beware of tablets with colour variations, unusual markings or incorrect labelling.

2. Use a marker test:

Some manufacturers provide marker tests for their products to help consumers verify the authenticity of tablets. These tests often involve the use of ultraviolet light to detect specific safety marks present on genuine tablets. Check the manufacturer's website to see if such tests are available for the Kamagra you have purchased.

3. Check the batch codes and expiry dates:

Authentic pharmaceutical products generally have batch codes and expiry dates clearly marked on the packaging. Make sure this information is present and check with the manufacturer if necessary to confirm its validity.

4. Compare with official images:

Consult the manufacturer's official website or other reliable sources for accurate images and descriptions of the genuine Kamagra product. Compare this information with what you received to identify potential differences that could indicate a counterfeit.

5. Seek the advice of a healthcare professional:

If you have any doubts about the authenticity of the Kamagra you have purchased, do not hesitate to consult a healthcare professional. They can help you assess the product and recommend appropriate measures if necessary.

By following these tips, you can increase your chances of receiving genuine and effective Kamagra to treat erectile dysfunction. Be vigilant and don't hesitate to take extra steps to ensure the safety and effectiveness of your treatment.
